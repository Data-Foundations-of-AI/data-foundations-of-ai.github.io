  <h1>Data Foundations of AI Social</h1>
  <p>An informal gathering for researchers interested in the data foundations of AI, hosted as part of the <a href="https://icml.cc/Conferences/2026/CallForSocials" class="text-link">ICML 2026 Social program</a>. ICML 2026 takes place at the COEX Convention &amp; Exhibition Center in Seoul, South Korea. Come meet the community, connect with fellow attendees, and join a panel discussion over light refreshments.</p>
